# Beirut Poly – Possible References

It seems “Beirut Polytechnic” (or **Beirut Poly**) can refer to different institutions or projects in and around Beirut.  

---

## 1. Polytech-Beirut – Faculty of Engineering (Université La Sagesse)
- The **Faculty of Engineering** at Sagesse University.  
- Known as **Polytech-Beirut**.  
- Mission: train engineers with expertise across major industry sectors.  
- Location: Furn El Chebbak, Beirut.  
- Part of La Sagesse University.  

---

## 2. Sagesse University – Polytech & Health Faculty Building
- A new construction project hosting **two faculties**: *Polytech* and *Health*.  
- Total area: ~13,200 m².  
- Includes: underground parking, lobby/auditorium, laboratories, administrative offices.  
- An extension of Sagesse/St. Paul University in Beirut.  

---

## 3. POLYTECH (Environmental & Biotech Company)
- Provides solutions in **bioengineering, water treatment, solid waste management, and bio-agriculture**.  
- Operates in the greater Beirut area.  

---

## 4. Polytech-Agro-Environment Technologies
- Established around **1975**, located in **Baabda** (close to Beirut).  
- Focus: **agro-environmental solutions** and sustainable technologies.  

---

## 5. Polytech Bioagriculture
- Lebanese **online store** for organic culinary and medicinal plants.  
- Located in **Baushrieh (Jdeideh, Beirut area)**.
