About Beirut Poly

Beirut Poly is an engineering and technology faculty based in Beirut, Lebanon. Our mission is to provide immersive, practice‑oriented education that bridges foundational theory with real‑world application. Students learn through industry projects, modern laboratories, and mentorship from faculty with strong research and field experience.

Programs & Tracks

Undergraduate and graduate paths designed with input from industry and research centers. Sample tracks below — customize curricula with electives and project‑based modules.

Computer & Software Engineering

Systems, AI/ML, cloud, embedded software. Team projects with version control, CI/CD, and secure coding practices.

  • AI & Data Systems
  • Web & Mobile
  • Cybersecurity
Electrical & Mechatronics

Power systems, control, robotics, and IoT. Work with PLCs, sensors, and rapid prototyping platforms.

  • Robotics & Control
  • Renewable Energy
  • IoT Systems
Mechanical & Industrial

Design for manufacture, CAD/CAM, additive manufacturing, and lean operations with real factory case studies.

  • Product Design
  • Manufacturing
  • Operations & QA
Civil & Environmental

Sustainable infrastructure, smart cities, water resources, and resilient urban planning for the Mediterranean context.

Biomedical Engineering

Medical devices, biosignals, and assistive technologies, co‑developed with local hospitals and clinics.

Business & Tech Management (Minor)

Product, entrepreneurship, and operations to help engineers launch startups and lead cross‑functional teams.
